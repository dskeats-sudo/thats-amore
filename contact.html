Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes! You can book a table online via OpenTable using the link above, or call us directly on 01444 484 824. We strongly recommend booking, especially at weekends.

Do you cater for dietary requirements?

Absolutely. Our menu has vegetarian (V), vegan (VG) and gluten-free (GF) options clearly marked. Please let our staff know of any allergies or requirements when booking or on arrival.

Is the restaurant family-friendly?

Yes, we love welcoming families. We have a children's menu available and are happy to accommodate pushchairs and high chairs. For larger family groups, please book in advance.

Do you offer takeaway or collection?

Yes! We offer collection — call us directly on 01444 484 824 to place a collection order. We'll have it ready and waiting for you.

Do you have a car park?

We're located on Lindfield High Street. There is public car parking nearby on the High Street and at the village car parks. We're easily accessible on foot from the village centre.

Do you offer special menus for occasions?

Yes, we offer a set menu (starter, main, dessert) which guests describe as excellent value, as well as our pizza party package for groups. Contact us to discuss your requirements.
